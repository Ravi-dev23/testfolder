Wildlife animals

what is wildlife

Wildlife refers to undomesticated animals and uncultivated plant species that live in their natural habitats without being introduced by humans.

Some wildlife animals




> The lion (Panthera leo) is a large cat of the genus Panthera, native to Sub-Saharan Africa and India.
